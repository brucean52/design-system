## Usage Guidelines
* Use as navigation within a larger section (ex. under an expand section or within a drawer)
* Do not put tabs within tabs

## Text Guidelines
For tab labels:
* Use [Title Case](https://elementary.io/docs/human-interface-guidelines#capitalization).
* Avoid punctuation.
* Use short, precise terms.
* Use discrete terms to clearly distinguish the content under each tab.
